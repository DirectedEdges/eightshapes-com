Save time and guarantee buy-in with this session to inventory a design. If your team is starting a library or large scale redesign, deconstruct your experience into reusable components you can plan and build with.

You will:

  • Use scissors, pens, papers, and Post-It flags and notes
  • Cut up screenshots to identify each component
  • Name, prioritize, and group each component you find
  • Work as small groups and then compare results
