Design Systems

Make your experience cohesive and your teams efficient with a design system of parts and practices to serve your product teams.

It’s not just a living style guide. Modern organizations invest in systems so they can deliver innovative and beautiful products, together. We can guide you.

Make It Modular

Designers are getting systematic with their work. They deliver systems as living, coded guides. Everyone’s thinking atomic and building modular.

EightShapes has 10+ years experience designing components, sustaining client libraries, and sharing what we know with 2009’s Modular Web Design and workshops worldwide.

What We Do

Discover & Plan

We assess opportunities, catalog your library, plan a formative period, and pair with you on the parts, products, and people.

2–4 weeks

Design & Build

We make the library with you or for you, from designing components & page concepts to building the living style guide.

2–3 months

Workshop

We kickoff efforts, sell the mission, teach concepts, and propel in-house teams forward with custom presentations and workshops.

1–5 days

Coach

We ask hard questions, respond to your ideas, and offer straight advice to improve your efforts, with just you or your whole team.

10 hr blocks
