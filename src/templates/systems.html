Design Systemss

All the parts and people serving a portfolio of products – make teams more efficient and experiences more cohesive.

It’s not just a style guide. Instead, modern enterprises invest in tools and practices so teams can make innovative, beautiful products together. EightShapes can guide you there.

Design’s future is modular. It’s always been components.

Designers code more and are delivering their work as a toolkit of interdependent parts. Thinking modularly is now a key trait for today’s high performing teams. Everyone’s thinking atomic.

EightShapes has been thinking in @components for years, helping clients start and sustain component libraries for nearly 10 years and sharing the practices with 2009’s Modular Web Design.
