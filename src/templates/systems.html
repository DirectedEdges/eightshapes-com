Design Systems

Parts and people serving a portfolio of products, making your teams more efficient and experiences more cohesive.

It’s not just a style guide. Today's enterprises invest in systems so teams can sustain innovative, beautiful products together. EightShapes can guide you.

It’s components time.

Designers and devs now modularize their work. They deliver systems as living, coded style guides. Everyone’s thinking atomic.

We've spent over 10 years designing in components, sustaining libraries for our clients, and sharing what we know with 2009’s Modular Web Design book and workshops worldwide.

What We Do

Discover & Plan

We can assess opportunities, catalog your library, plan a formative period, and pair with you on the parts, products, and people.

2–4 weeks

Design & Build

2–3 months

Workshop

Coach

Workshops

Intro to Design Systems

Sustaining Design Systems
