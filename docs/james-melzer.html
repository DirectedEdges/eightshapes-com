James Melzer

Director, UX
Eightshapes since 2009

As a UX designer and team lead, James connects business leaders, designers and developers to design the best solutions. James’ 15 years of experience includes large-scale information architecture, search, responsive design, and creating living style guides for design teams. His previous clients include startups, Fortune 500 enterprises, non-profits, government agencies, and a university. James earned a Master’s Degree in Library/Information Science from the University of Maryland.
