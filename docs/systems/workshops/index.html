Design Systems Workshops

Looking to jump start a design system program? We workshop with team(s) to teach methods, conduct actvities, and plan a future to get the most out of a system.

What We Cover

Design systems cover alot of ground. Architecting a high-quality library of design, code and documentation is essential. So to is operating a program, from forming a vision and roadmap to aligning people, processes, and contributions from a wide community.

EightShapes will customize a workshop for you that blends and prioritizes topics across a day or two together. For each topic, we'll find the right balance of lecture, hands-on activities, discussion, and – if you're ready – critique of your team's materials. If a default agenda suits your needs, excellent! Need to focus on some topics more than others? We'll customize an agenda to suit your needs.

One client paired a sponsored talk for a local meetup with a customized half-day in-house workshop. Their objectives were to align multiple system efforts and better serve system customers and contributors. As a result, they omitted most topics of design, code and documentation.

Prepping for the Day

Once you're signed up, we’ll meet at least three times to plan the event. For workshops requiring customization or additional consulting, we'll add activities as needed — interviews, more tours, etc — so that we can present material most effectively for your audience.

Meeting 1 (60 min)

Objectives, Topics and Draft Agenda

We'll workshop the workshop, drafting objectives, themes, topics, activities, and what excites each of us.

Meeting 2 (90 min)

Design System and Experience Tours

EightShapes quickly immerses in your system's existing design, code and doc and tours the products it's used for.

Meeting 3 (60 min)

Final Agenda, Materials and Logistics

We'll confirm the day's agenda, any materials and custom content to be produced, and finalize logistics.
