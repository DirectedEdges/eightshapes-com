Workshops

Looking to start or reinvigorate a design system? We’ll workshop with your group to teach individuals and align teams pursuing design systems.

Why a Workshop with EightShapes?

With EightShapes’ seasoned experience and enthusiasm, you’ll inspire your teams and spark systems work. Your team can use a workshop to:

    • Kickoff or energize in-house systems efforts
    • Define your system and what it offers
    • Refine how to design and code system features
    • Roadmap how to create and adopt a system
    • Develop a common vocabulary for your teams
    • Produce meaningful outcomes and teach concepts with engaging hands-on activities

What We Cover

During one or two days together, we’ll combine lecture and hands-on activities on how to plan, design, document, operate and adopt a system.

If the standard, out-of-the-box agenda suits your needs, excellent! Otherwise, we’ll work with you to tailor topics and hands-on activities to your current needs.

For public events and organizations looking for general education, we’ll follow a standard agenda that hits all the primary topics.

In one instance, a company paired an sponsored talk to the local community with a customized half-day in-house workshop. They chose to focus on aligning multiple in-house system efforts around a future strategy along topics covering how to practice systems. As a result, they omitted topics of system design, code and documentation.

Who Should You Invite?

The systems workshop provides content relevant to many disciplines and is suitable for as many as 80 participants.

  • Required Participants

    As a system bridges design and engineering, you should at least invite:

    • Assigned staff already making system(s)
    • UX and visual designers
    • Front-end engineers
    • Design and engineering leaders

  • Recommended Participants

    Systems impact an entire organization, so the workshop is even better with:

    • Content strategists
    • Researchers
    • Product managers
    • Executive sponsors

How We Prepare

Once we sign an agreement, we’ll meet three times over one to two weeks.

Over two of those sessions, we’ll set objectives, agenda and logistics. The third sessions provides for a quick, hour-long tour for your team to immerse us in your relevant products and systems already in use.

Workshop Customization

While we’ll start from preexisting material used for at public conferences, we can also customize materials to focus conversation with optional additional activities billed hourly. These activities can include:

  • Interviews

    Interview system stakeholders to understand priorities, culture, and existing practices.

  • System Reviews

    Dig into your as-is systems documentation, design assets, and code.

  • Customize Materials

    Develop client-specific slides & stories, integrating relevant quotes, product and system examples.
