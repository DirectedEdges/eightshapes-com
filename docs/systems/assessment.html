Design System
Assessments

Interested in checking the health of your design system? We’ll assess your system using proven heuristics to identify the most important challenges you face going forward.

Why an Assessment with EightShapes?

Every design system can function better, offer more refined features, and improve how it works. EightShapes' experience with over design systems offers a nuanced view and sensitivity for the cost and benefit of choices you'll face.

  • Learn what's working and not working
  • Celebrate areas of high performance
  • Name and prioritize challenges ahead
  • Understand topics needing improvement
  • Trigger discussions of team and community
  • Increase operational efficiencies
  • Fill gaps that improve quality
  • Foster alignment on next steps

What You Get

Combine outputs you refer to for months with outcomes you benefit from for years.

Assessment Report

You'll receive a 70 to 100 slide presentation report that summarizes heuristic results and threads findings and recommendations through success and challenge themes.

Assessment Data

You'll receive raw interview notes and in-depth heuristics scored for performance, urgency (by Nathan and your team), findings, recommendations, and theme categories.

Coaching Critiques

We'll convene for up to four hour-long coaching sessions. You bring the topic—emerging work, established solution, or process— and Nathan and other teammates critique.

Workshop (Optional)

When relevant, you can conclude an assessment with an onsite workshop to present the report, dive deep on important topics, and engage your community in hands-on activities.
