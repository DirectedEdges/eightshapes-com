Design Systems
Workshop

Plan Parts, Products, & People

Duration
1/4 day
Participants
4 to 40

Most see a system as parts: visual style, UI components, editorial, research, code, and more. But to create a scalable system, you also plan for the people and products involved. We’ll lead exercises and provoke discussion to kickstart your system efforts.

What You Do

  • Expose what parts each participant values
  • Identify and prioritize products
  • Explore how to organize the system
  • Compare and aggregate inputs using worksheets
  • Uncover lack of alignment, personal biases, and trends

What You Get

  • Focused priorities on what to do first versus pursue later
  • Insight on who’d best contribute and participate
  • A sense of tiered importance of each product
  • Ideas of flagship products to normalize first
  • Followup conversations needed to align teams and execs

How We Customize

  • Integrate examples of your design into lecture slides to spur discussion
  • Refactor worksheet choices biased towards your system’s needs
