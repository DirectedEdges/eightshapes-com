What We Do

Drawing on time-tested design techniques, EightShapes runs a discovery phase that emphasizes exploration, iteration, and validation.

1 Frame the Problem

Solutions depend on understanding the problem. We work together to understand the constraints, parameters, and objectives that define the problem space.

2 Gather Insights

Design concepts propose solutions that might solve the problem. Converting a problem statement into testable hypotheses is a crucial step in driving design and research activities.

3 Prototype Ideas

After brainstorming and iterating in low-fidelity sketches, we work together to develop a prototype that represents the concept and allows us to test our hypotheses.

4 Test Solutions

Running the prototype in front of the target audience gives answers to the hypotheses but also provides insights about the users.

Brainstorming & Ideation Techniques

Sketching Workshop

Bring the whole team together for a few hours to sketch ideas, which fuels the creative process, and sheds light on diverse concerns and requirements.

Affinity Mapping

To identify priorities and surface assumptions, participants answer a key question then work together to group and prioritize their insights.

Application Modeling

Drawing a map of the entire application gives us a holistic view of the product, helping to plan detailed design phases.

Research & Testing Techniques

Discovery depends on research to inform and validate the product vision. These are some of the lightweight techniques we use to inject user insights into design.

Usability Testing

Testing your product prioritizes fixes to the UI and clarifies what's important to users.

Navigation Testing

Card sorts and “treejack” tests provide insights about labeling, structure, and priorities.

First-Click Testing

Asking users to click on a mock-up to complete a task offers insights about UI effectiveness.
