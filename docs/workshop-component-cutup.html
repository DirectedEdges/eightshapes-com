Cut Up a Component Inventory

Duration
Up to 1/2 Day
Participants
4 to 40

Save time and guarantee buy-in with this session to inventory an experience. If your team is starting a library or large scale redesign, deconstruct your experience into reusable components you can plan and build with.

What You Do

  • Use scissors, pens, papers, and Post-It flags and notes
  • Cut up screenshots to identify each component
  • Name, prioritize, and group each component you find
  • Work as small groups and then compare results

What You Get

  • An organized library inventory, discovered together
  • Priorities of which components matter
  • An energized team anxious for you to build the library!

How We Customize

  • Integrate examples of your design into slides to spur discussion
  • Prep and print screenshots of your UX, across platforms
  • Vary team composition to group individuals strategically

Background

The workshop was described in detail in the 2009 book Modular Web Design, now reprinted in a medium.com Component Cut Up article.
