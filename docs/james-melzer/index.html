As a UX designer and team lead, James connects business leaders, designers and developers to design the best solutions. James’ 18 years of experience includes large-scale information architecture, search, responsive design, and creating living style guides for design teams.

James has led accounts and projects with diverse clients in industry, education, government, and civil society, on projects such as:

  • Led a design system team that helped a financial institution realize its efficiency and quality goals.
  • Built and governed a design system to achieve brand cohesiveness across dozens of vendor and in-house teams with over a hundred native apps.
  • Facilitated c-suite ideation toward an app's MVP, then led the team to realize the concept via prototypes.
  • Led the research and design team to overhaul the federal government's assistance for victims of identity theft.

James designed navigation schemes for several of the world's largest companies and organizations. He relishes thorny information architecture problems, leveraging years of experience and a Master’s Degree in Library/Information Science from the University of Maryland.
